Frequently Asked Questions

Does AI diagnose conditions?

No. AI is restricted to triage outputs only: risk level, safe actions, and visit recommendation.

Can AI provide medicine names or dosage?

No. Medication and treatment instructions are blocked by policy and output filters.

What if red flags are detected?

The system returns RED risk immediately and shows an emergency warning to seek urgent care.

Which roles are supported?

Owner, Doctor, and Clinic Admin, each with role-specific dashboards and permissions.

Is this compatible with Tilda?

Yes. Every file is plain HTML/CSS/JS and can be pasted into Tilda HTML blocks.
