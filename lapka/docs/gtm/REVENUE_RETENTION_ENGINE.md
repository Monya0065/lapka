# Revenue + Retention Engine (Phase 2)

## Objective

Move from pilot wins to repeatable growth across clinic chains.

## Revenue Engine

- Value pricing based on branch throughput and no-show reduction impact.
- Expansion motion: activate one branch, then scale to sibling branches.
- Monthly business review with ROI deltas from `OUTCOME_METRICS_TREE`.

## Retention Engine

- Weekly health score per clinic:
  - WAU trend
  - protocol completion trend
  - no-show risk trend
  - unresolved critical alerts
- Churn prevention playbook:
  - low usage intervention
  - workflow enablement session
  - executive sponsor escalation

## Operating Cadence

- Weekly: pipeline + retention risk review.
- Monthly: revenue + outcome review.
- Quarterly: expansion and renewal strategy.
